About AEC & TROCSA

Established in 2014, as part of the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education (CAPE). It is the 8th Engineering College under the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education. TROCSA is a initiative of CS department where the students can develop their skills in various level of fields that helps in their career.
